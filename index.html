Privacy Policy — Minku's Learning App

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Minku's Learning App ("we", "our", or "us") is an educational app designed for children aged 4–6. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • No name, email, or contact details collected
  • No location data collected
  • No photos, camera, or microphone access
  • No account registration required
  • No data shared with third parties

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is directed to children under 13. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app works completely offline and requires no internet connection to function.

4. Data Storage

The only data stored is app progress (levels completed, content unlocked) which is saved locally on the device only. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible by us.

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases to unlock additional learning content:

  • Purchases are processed securely through the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • In-app purchase transactions are governed by Google Play's Terms of Service and billing policies.
  • Purchase records are managed by Google and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • All purchases must be made by parents or guardians. We encourage parents to manage in-app purchase settings on their device to prevent unintended purchases by children.
  • Refund requests should be submitted through Google Play.

6. Internet Usage

The App requires internet access only for the following optional features:

  • In-app purchases — to process and verify purchases via Google Play Billing.
  • YouTube button — an optional link that opens our educational YouTube channel externally in a browser. No YouTube data is collected within the App itself.

Core learning content (numbers, alphabet, colors, shapes, fruits, vegetables, body parts) works fully offline.

7. Advertising

Minku's Learning App contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not integrate any advertising SDKs or display ads to users.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Billing — for processing in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube — optional external link only. No data is collected within the App.

We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services.

9. Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review locally stored data by uninstalling the App, which removes all local data from the device.
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns at the email below.
  • Request information about our data practices at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
