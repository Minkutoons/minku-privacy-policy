Privacy Policy — Minku's Learning App

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Minku's Learning App ("we", "our", or "us") is an educational app designed for children aged 4–6. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • No name, email, or contact details collected
  • No location data collected
  • No photos, camera, or microphone access
  • No account registration required
  • No data shared with third parties

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is directed to children under 13. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app works completely offline and requires no internet connection to function.

4. Data Storage

The only data stored is app progress (levels completed, scores) which is saved locally on the device only. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible by us.

5. Advertising

Minku's Learning App contains no advertisements of any kind.

6. Third Party Services

Minku's Learning App does not use any third party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
